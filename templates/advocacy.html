Advocacy Ministry

This is the voice of the church. The Advocacy ministry is under the SWEM sub-committee and it's responsible for speaking out and taking action on behalf of individuals or groups who are oppressed or unable to stand for themselves. Some functions of this ministry include:

  1. Raising Awareness: Educating the community and the broader public about social justice issues, such as poverty, human rights, and environmental stewardship, from a Christian perspective.
  2. Supporting the Vulnerable: Offering support to those who are disadvantaged or facing injustice, including providing resources, guidance, and a platform for their voices to be heard.
  3. Influencing Policy: Engaging with policymakers and government officials to promote laws and policies that reflect Christian values of justice, compassion, and care for the less fortunate.
  4. Encouraging Action: Mobilizing the Christian community to take tangible actions, such as participating in campaigns, signing petitions, or volunteering, to address the issues identified by the ministry.
