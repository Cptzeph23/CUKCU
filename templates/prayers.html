Prayer Schedule

Prayer is at the heart of our Christian journey. Join us as we seek God's presence, intercede for our community, and grow in faith together.

Weekly Prayer Meetings

  • Monday, Tuesday & Thursday: Hall 4, CUK Campus or resident students

Prayer & Fasting Fridays

Every Friday, we dedicate ourselves at the Prayer Garden for prayer and fasting, seeking God's guidance and renewal.

  • 10:00 AM: Morning session
  • 1:00 PM: Midday session
  • 4:00 PM: Closing session

Why We Pray

  • Spiritual Growth: Deepen our faith and connection with God.
  • Intercession: Stand in the gap for the church, campus, and nation.
  • Strength & Renewal: Find peace, direction, and empowerment.
  • Unity: Join fellow believers in a powerful time of worship and supplication.
