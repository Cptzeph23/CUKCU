Gentlemen's Ministry

The Gentlemen's Fellowship is a vibrant community where the men of the church come together to grow spiritually, strengthen bonds, and support one another in faith, leadership, and service.

Core Activities

  • Weekly Fellowships: Engaging Bible studies and discussions on faith and leadership.
  • Prayer Meetings: Dedicated sessions to pray for one another, the church, and society.
  • Mentorship Programs: Encouraging young men to develop spiritually, academically, and professionally.
  • Community Service: Engaging in charitable activities and outreach programs.
  • Men’s Retreats: Special events for spiritual renewal, bonding, and relaxation.

Fellowship Meetings

The Gentlemen's Fellowship meets every Tuesday from 5:00 PM to 6:00 PM Virtually or at an agreed upon location. These meetings include:

  • Scripture Sharing: Studying the Word and its application in daily life.
  • Testimonies: Sharing personal experiences and spiritual journeys.
  • Workshops: Learning practical skills for leadership and personal development.

Key Initiatives

  • Men’s Welfare Fund: Supporting members in need, including financial aid and emergency assistance.
  • Leadership Development: Equipping men with skills to lead in ministry, family, and society.
  • Brotherhood Bonding Events: Sports activities, hikes, and team-building sessions.
  • Evangelism & Outreach: Reaching out to other men with the Gospel.

How to Join

  • Attend the weekly fellowship meetings.
  • Register with the gentlemen leader during CU sign-ups.
  • Engage in group activities and contribute to the welfare of the members.
  • Take part in mentorship and discipleship programs.
