    Bible Study

    CUKCU has organized Bible study groups for students to come together and share the word of God guided by Bible study guides provided by FOCUS kenya. The groups are formed according to various places of residences within Gataka and school.

    Prayers

    For spiritual growth and personal encounter with God, CUKCU has partnerd with Redeemed Gospel church-Gataka where every evening from 7:00PM to 8:30Pm students gather at this church to pray.

    Praise and Worship.

    "God is a spirit and those who worship him must worship Him in Spirit and in truth. [John 4:24]"
    Therefore, CUKCU has designated Thursday from 4pma to 6pm and Saturday from 4pm to 6pm as days to practice songs for praise and worship during the Sunday service

    Leadership

    For better running of the church, CUKCU provides leadership roles to eligible and elected members to carry out leadership practices in various ministries and dockets
