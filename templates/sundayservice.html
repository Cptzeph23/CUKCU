Sunday Service

Sunday is our official day of worship, prayer, and fellowship. It is a day dedicated to seeking God's presence, growing in faith, and experiencing the power of communal worship.

Sunday Service Schedule

  • Morning Devotion: 7:30 AM - 8:30 AM
  • Quiet Time: 8:30 AM - 8:45 AM
  • Main Service: 8:45 AM - 11:30 AM

The Essence of Sunday Worship

Our Sunday services focus on worship, the preaching of the Word, intercessory prayer, and community fellowship.

Key Activities

  • Praise & Worship: Lifting our hearts to God in song and adoration.
  • Preaching of the Word: Learning and growing through biblical teachings.
  • Intercessory Prayers: Praying for the church, individuals, and the community.
  • Testimonies & Thanksgiving: Sharing how God has been working in our lives.
  • Holy Communion: Observed periodically as a remembrance of Christ’s sacrifice.

Join Us

Be part of our vibrant Sunday worship experience. Come and encounter God’s presence as we fellowship together.
