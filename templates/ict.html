ICT Ministry

The ICT Ministry plays a crucial role in ensuring smooth church operations through technology, managing sound systems, projections, live streaming, and all digital communication.

Our Role in the Church

  • Sound System Management: Ensuring clear audio during services and events.
  • Instrument Setup: Assisting in setting up and maintaining musical instruments.
  • Projection & Multimedia: Managing PowerPoint presentations, videos, and scripture displays.
  • Live Streaming & Recording: Broadcasting services online and handling media archives.
  • Technical Support: Assisting with ICT needs during services and church programs.

Activities & Services

  • Sunday Service Support: Handling sound, projections, and digital needs.
  • Event Coverage: Providing audio-visual and streaming services for special events.
  • Equipment Maintenance: Ensuring all ICT tools function optimally.
  • Poster Making: Designing posters for chuch events
  • Church Website & Social Media: Managing online presence and digital outreach.

Key Responsibilities

  • Enhancing Worship Experience: Ensuring quality sound and visuals.
  • Training Volunteers: Equipping members with ICT and media skills.
  • Upgrading Technology: Keeping church tech up to date.
  • Cyber Security & Data Management: Protecting church digital assets.

How to Join

  • Sign up during ministry registration or visit the media team.
  • Attend technical training sessions to equip yourself with necessary skills.
  • Serve actively in sound management, projections, and live streaming.
  • Stay updated with the latest church technology and improvements.

    • Contact the ICT coordinator for more information
