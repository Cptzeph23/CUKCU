Order of Service

  1. Intercessory : A time of Prayer and invitation of The Holy Spirit as the service begins. 6:30PM - 6:45PM
  2. Praise & Worship: A time of uplifting songs and worship. 6:45PM - 7:15PM
  3. Offering: An opportunity to give and support the ministry. 7:15PM - 7:30PM
  4. Speaker IntroductionThe host of the day reads the speaker's profile and welcomes him/her and prays for the offerings . 7:30PM - 7:45PM
  5. Sermon: A message delivered by invited speakers. 7:45PM - 7:20PM
  6. Conclusion and Closing Prayer: The chairperson or the vice chair concludes the service with short prayer

Unlike the Sunday Service, the Friday Service is shorter with fewer activities, allowing attendees to receive the Word effectively in a concise format.

Key Highlights

  • Invited Speakers: Special guest ministers bring powerful sermons.
  • Spirit-filled Worship: Led by the praise and worship team.
  • Focused Teaching: A short but impactful message centered on biblical principles.

How to Get Involved

  • Attend regularly and invite friends.
  • Join the worship team or volunteer in service activities.
  • Engage in discussions and share testimonies.
