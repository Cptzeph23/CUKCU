Worship Ministry

The Worship Ministry is responsible for coordinating all praise and worship sessions, ensuring that the music aligns with the spiritual standards of the Christian Union.

Roles & Responsibilities

  • Leading Worship: Conducting praise and worship sessions during services and events.
  • Music Selection: Choosing songs that align with biblical teachings and church doctrine.
  • Rehearsals & Training: Organizing practice sessions to maintain musical excellence.
  • Instrument Coordination: Ensuring all musical instruments are well-maintained and played skillfully.
  • Choir & Team Management: Recruiting and mentoring worship team members.

Practice

Music Practices occur every Thursday and Friday in Hall 4

Thursday 4:00PM - 5:00PM: Choir practice

Thursday 5:00PM - 6:00PM: Praise and Worship practice

Saturday 4:00PM - 5:00PM: Choir practice

Saturday 4:00PM - 6:00PM: Praise and Worship practice

Our Mission

To create a worship atmosphere that invites the presence of God, uplifts believers, and glorifies His name.

How to Join

If you are passionate about worship and music, you are welcome to join the Worship Ministry. Attend rehearsals and serve in the worship team.
