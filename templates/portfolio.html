CUK Christian Union Portfolio

The Christian Union at the Co-operative University of Kenya (CUK) is a fellowship of believers dedicated to spiritual growth, evangelism, and community service. Our mission is To Develop Holistic Students Through Evangelism, Discipleship, Leadership Development and Organizational Effectiveness

Our Mission & Vision

  • Mission: To Develop Holistic Students Through Evangelism, Discipleship, Leadership Development and Organizational Effectiveness
  • Vision: Christian Students Transforming The University, Church and Society.

Ministries & Activities

  • Kesha: Organizing various Kesha nights such as Worship kesha, Prayer kesha etc.
  • Praise & Worship: Leading the congregation in worship sessions.
  • Evangelism: Spreading the gospel within and beyond the campus.
  • Discipleship: Mentoring and nurturing spiritual growth.
  • Community Outreach: Engaging in charity work and helping the needy.
  • Bible Study: Deepening biblical knowledge and understanding.

Our Achievements

  • Established a strong worship team and choir.
  • Successfully hosted annual mission weeks.
  • Provided scholarships and support to needy students.
  • Expanded community outreach programs.
  • Organized leadership training for student leaders.
  • Successfully conducted fund drive for the purchase of new Music intruments
  • Bought New Music instruments which improved th worship and praise experience

How to Join

  • Attend our weekly fellowship meetings.
  • Participate in ministry activities and training sessions.
  • Engage in outreach and mission work.
  • Connect with CU leaders for mentorship and guidance.

    • For more details, contact the Christian Union leadership team.
