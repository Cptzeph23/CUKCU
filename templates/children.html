Children's Ministry

The Children's Ministry is a vibrant and joyful ministry of our union, focused on nurturing the spiritual growth of children through biblical teachings, interactive activities, and a strong sense of community. Our mission is to guide children in their journey of faith and help them grow into Christ-like individuals.

We believe in creating a safe, engaging, and fun environment where children can explore their faith, learn valuable life lessons, and build lasting friendships.

Activities in the Children's Ministry

Bible Study

Interactive and age-appropriate Bible study sessions that teach children about God's love and the stories of the Bible.

Sunday School

Engaging Sunday school classes with songs, storytelling, and group discussions tailored to children.

Community Outreach

Opportunities for children to participate in outreach activities, teaching them the value of service and compassion.

Join the Children's Ministry

We welcome all children from ages 4 to 12 to be part of our ministry. Parents are encouraged to get involved and support their children's spiritual journey. For more information, reach out to the Children's Ministry coordinator at the church office.
