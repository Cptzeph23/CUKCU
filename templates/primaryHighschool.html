Primary & High School Ministry

The Primary & High School Ministry is dedicated to spreading the gospel to students in nearby schools through evangelism and mentorship.

Evangelism & Ministry Sessions

Every Friday, members of the Christian Union visit nearby primary and high schools to conduct spiritual guidance sessions.

  • PPI Sessions: Conducted in primary schools to teach biblical values and encourage young believers.
  • High School Ministry: Engaging students through worship, testimonies, and biblical teaching.
  • Mentorship Programs: Providing moral and spiritual guidance to students.

Our Mission

To nurture young minds in faith, inspire them to follow Christ, and equip them with biblical knowledge for their daily lives.

Why We Minister to Schools

  • Spiritual Growth: Encouraging students to develop a relationship with Christ.
  • Character Building: Instilling Christian values in young people.
  • Leadership Training: Preparing students to be future Christian leaders.
  • Community Outreach: Strengthening the bond between the Christian Union and the local community.

How to Get Involved

Join us every Friday as we minister to students and spread the love of Christ. For more details, connect with the Primary & High School Ministry team.
