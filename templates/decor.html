Decor Ministry

The Decor Ministry brings life and vibrancy to the Christian Union through creative and eye-catching designs. From stage decorations to entrance displays, the ministry ensures every physical space reflects beauty and order, creating an environment that fosters worship, fellowship, and inspiration.

Our team works with excellence, blending colors, themes, and artistic elements to make every event and gathering visually captivating and spiritually uplifting.

Our Vision

To create stunning, welcoming, and spiritually inspiring environments that glorify God and enhance worship experiences.

Our Activities

  • Stage Decoration: Designing and arranging stage backdrops, banners, and props for services, events, and productions.
  • Entrance Styling: Setting up welcoming entrances with flowers, arches, and themed displays for special occasions.
  • Event Themes: Crafting decorations based on specific themes for conferences, missions, and celebrations.
  • Seasonal Decor: Beautifying spaces during Christmas, Easter, and other festive seasons to reflect the message and mood of the time.
  • Workshops: Training members in design techniques, color coordination, and creative decor methods.

Our Tools and Materials

The Decor Ministry uses a variety of tools and materials, including:

  • Fabrics (e.g., drapes, linens)
  • Flowers (natural and artificial)
  • Lighting fixtures for ambiance
  • Props and accessories for themes
  • Craft supplies like glue, ribbons, and paints
Our team ensures all resources are used creatively and responsibly to produce outstanding results.
