Praise & Worship Ministry

The Praise & Worship Ministry is committed to leading the congregation in heartfelt worship and praise. Through music and song, we create an atmosphere for God's presence and prepare hearts for His word.

Our Role in the Church

  • Leading Worship: Guiding the congregation in songs of praise and worship.
  • Music Selection: Choosing songs that align with the theme of the service.
  • Instrumental Support: Providing live music through skilled instrumentalists.
  • Vocal Training: Training members in vocal techniques and harmony.
  • Spiritual Preparation: Praying and fasting to seek God's guidance in worship.

Praise & Worship Activities

  • Sunday Worship Services: Leading worship sessions during church services.
  • Midweek Worship: Conducting worship sessions during Bible study and prayer meetings.
  • Music Workshops: Training sessions for vocalists and instrumentalists.
  • Concerts & Worship Nights: Organizing special worship events.
  • Choir & Band Rehearsals: Regular practice to ensure excellence in worship.

How to Join

  • Sign up during ministry registration.
  • Attend training sessions and rehearsals.
  • Commit to personal and spiritual growth in worship.
  • Dedicate time for practice and prayer.

    • For more information, contact the Praise & Worship Ministry coordinator.
