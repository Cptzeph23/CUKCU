Hospitality Ministry

The Hospitality Ministry is dedicated to extending love, warmth, and care to all members and visitors of the church. We believe that hospitality is a vital aspect of Christian service, ensuring that everyone feels welcomed, valued, and at home.

Our Role in the Church

  • Welcoming Guests: Greeting visitors with kindness and assisting them in finding their way.
  • Ushers & Greeters: Organizing seating, guiding members, and ensuring smooth service flow.
  • Care & Support: Reaching out to new and existing members in times of joy and sorrow.
  • Organizing Refreshments: Providing snacks and beverages after services and events.

Activities & Services

  • Visitor Follow-up: Ensuring that first-time visitors receive a warm follow-up and invitation to return.
  • Hospital & Home Visits: Offering comfort and encouragement to those in need.
  • Event Hosting: Assisting in the preparation and hospitality of church functions.
  • Special Care: Supporting families during weddings, funerals, and special occasions.

Key Responsibilities

  • Creating a Warm Atmosphere: Making everyone feel welcome in the house of God.
  • Encouraging Fellowship: Connecting members to build strong Christian relationships.
  • Coordinating Volunteers: Training and organizing volunteers to assist in various hospitality duties.
  • Serving with Joy: Demonstrating Christâ€™s love through acts of kindness and service.

How to Join

  • Sign up during ministry registration or visit the information desk.
  • Attend training sessions to equip yourself for effective service.
  • Actively participate in hospitality events and services.
  • Serve with love, dedication, and a cheerful heart.
