The Ladies Ministry is dedicated to the spiritual growth, empowerment, and welfare of women in the Christian Union. Through fellowship, mentorship, and service, we nurture faith, support one another, and contribute to the wellness of the church.

Our Role in the Church

  • Spiritual Growth: Organizing prayer meetings, Bible studies, and discipleship programs.
  • Mentorship & Counseling: Providing guidance and support to young women in the Christian Union.
  • Church Welfare: Participating in hospitality, visitations, and care for the needy.
  • Community Outreach: Engaging in charity work and evangelism.
  • Personal Development: Hosting workshops on career growth, financial management, and health awareness.

Activities & Services

  • Fellowship Meetings: Weekly gatherings for prayer, worship, and sharing.
  • Retreats & Conferences: Organizing special events for spiritual renewal and bonding.
  • Entrepreneurship & Skills Training: Empowering women with business and craft skills.
  • Health & Wellness Programs: Promoting physical, emotional, and mental well-being.
  • Church Service Involvement: Leading in worship, ushering, and intercessory ministry.
