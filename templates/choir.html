Choir Ministry

The Choir Ministry is a vibrant and integral part of the Christian Union, dedicated to leading worship and spreading the gospel through music. This ministry is open to individuals passionate about singing, playing musical instruments, and using their talents for the glory of God.

With a mission to lead the congregation into a deeper experience of worship, the Choir Ministry also provides opportunities for members to grow spiritually, musically, and in fellowship with one another.

Our Vision

To raise a generation of worshippers who minister to God and inspire others through Spirit-led music and unwavering commitment to excellence in worship.

Activities and Responsibilities

  • Worship Leading: Leading praise and worship sessions during Sunday services, fellowship meetings, and special events.

  • Choir Practice: Regular rehearsals to prepare for worship sessions, learn new songs, and improve musical skills.

  • Outreach Ministry: Ministering in external events such as weddings, funerals, and community outreach programs.

  • Music Training: Offering training in vocals, musical instruments, and sound systems to equip members for excellence.

  • Songwriting: Composing and arranging original songs that reflect the ministry’s mission and values.

  • Recording Projects: Producing recordings for distribution within and beyond the Christian Union.

Membership

Membership in the Choir Ministry is open to anyone with a heart for worship and a willingness to serve. Members undergo auditions to assess their musical skills and are guided in areas of growth. New members are encouraged to participate in orientation programs to familiarize themselves with the ministry's vision and mission.

Annual Highlights

  • Worship Nights: Organizing and leading worship nights for the Christian Union and the larger community.

  • Choir Retreats: Hosting annual retreats focused on spiritual growth, team-building, and planning for the ministry.

  • Concerts: Hosting and participating in gospel concerts to showcase musical talents and spread the message of Christ.

  • Mission Tours: Joining missions and crusades to spread the gospel through music.

How to Join

Interested in joining the Choir Ministry? Here’s how you can become a part of this wonderful team:

  1. Sign up during our membership drive or contact the Choir Coordinator for details.
  2. Attend the audition session to assess your musical abilities.
  3. Participate in the orientation program to understand the ministry’s values, expectations, and commitments.
  4. Attend rehearsals and begin actively serving in worship sessions and events.
